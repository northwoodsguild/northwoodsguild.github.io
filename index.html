The Northwoods Folk & Trade Guild

SUBJECT: VESTER, James "Halyard"
BORN: 1930, Minnesota
RECORDS: 16mm Film / Magnetic Audio Tape
LOCATION: Lake 42, Cook County, MN

Jim "Halyard" Vester spent fifteen years on the iron ore carriers of the Great Lakes before coming inland in 1964. These recovered reels document his intention to construct the Guild schoolhouse and his philosophy of "The Steady Hand".

The Guild exists to ensure the songs of the North and the skills of the woodsman are not lost to the static of the modern age.
