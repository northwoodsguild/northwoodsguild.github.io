SUBJECT: SJOBERG, James "Halyard"
ORIGIN: Lake Superior / Inland Lake 42
RECORDS: 16mm Film & Magnetic Tape Transcripts
STATUS: Digitized for Preservation

Jim "Halyard" Sjoberg—his name meaning Sea-Mountain—lived between the two worlds of Northern Minnesota. After fifteen years as a wheelsman on the Big Lake, he moved inland to the rocky shores of Lake 42.

These logs document his transition from the "Sea" to the "Mountain," preserving the manual trades and quiet philosophies that defined the 1965 Northwoods frontier.
